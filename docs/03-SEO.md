# SEO — Jimsolutions Technology

## 1. Primary SEO goals
- Rank for local service intent searches in Lagos
- Improve visibility for electrical and solar installation keywords
- Build authority for appliance, kitchen, and interior-related searches
- Support AI search and answer engine understanding

## 2. Primary keyword themes
- electrician in Lagos
- electrical services Lagos
- solar installation Lagos
- inverter installation Lagos
- home appliances Lagos
- kitchen installation Lagos
- interior design Nigeria
- laundry room setup
- solar company Nigeria
- electrical contractor Nigeria

## 3. Suggested page title patterns
- Home: Jimsolutions Technology | Electrical, Solar & Home Solutions in Lagos
- About: About Jimsolutions Technology | Trusted Electrical and Solar Experts
- Services: Services | Electrical, Solar, Appliances, Kitchens & Interiors
- Projects: Projects | Jimsolutions Technology Work and Installations
- Gallery: Gallery | Jimsolutions Technology
- FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions | Jimsolutions Technology
- Contact: Contact Jimsolutions Technology | WhatsApp, Call, Email

## 4. Meta description guidance
Keep every description under about 155 to 160 characters, clear and natural, and include the main service focus plus location coverage.

## 5. Content strategy
- Each service should have its own page
- Each page should answer one clear search intent
- Use simple headings and direct benefits
- Include trust-building copy near the CTA
- Add FAQs to service pages where useful
- Avoid keyword stuffing

## 6. Local SEO elements
- Business name consistency
- Address consistency
- Phone number consistency
- WhatsApp consistency
- Service area mention: Lagos and nationwide
- Business hours mention: 8am to 10pm daily
- Google Business Profile alignment

## 7. Schema recommendations
- Organization
- LocalBusiness
- Service
- FAQPage
- BreadcrumbList
- WebSite
- WebPage

## 8. Social and sharing
- Add Open Graph images
- Add Twitter card support
- Make shared links attractive on WhatsApp and social media

## 9. Copy tone
- Professional
- Human
- Clear
- Trustworthy
- Local
- Not overly corporate
- Not obviously AI-generated

## 10. Sample testimonials guidance
Use sample testimonials only for draft layout testing. Replace before launch unless approved by the client.

Example style:
- The team was professional and the installation was neat.
- Communication was fast and helpful.
- They handled the work with care and delivered on time.

## 11. Sample project description guidance
Use short project cards and longer case-study style descriptions later if the client wants more SEO content.

Example style:
- Residential Solar Installation in Lagos
- Electrical Wiring Upgrade for a Family Home
- Kitchen Appliance Setup for a Modern Apartment
