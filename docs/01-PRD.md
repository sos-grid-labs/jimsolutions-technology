# PRD — Jimsolutions Technology Website

## 1. Product summary
Jimsolutions Technology needs a modern business website that presents the company as a trusted provider of electrical services, solar installation, home appliances, kitchen setup, interior design, and laundry space solutions.

The website should be built primarily to generate leads through WhatsApp and phone calls, while also improving Google visibility and giving the business a professional online presence.

## 2. Business goals
- Present the company clearly and professionally
- Convert visitors into WhatsApp leads
- Make the business look established and trustworthy
- Support SEO for Lagos and nationwide searches
- Showcase services, projects, gallery images, and FAQs
- Make it easy for the client to update content later

## 3. Target audience
- Homeowners
- Families looking for appliance supply and installation
- People needing electrical repairs or installation
- Customers interested in solar and inverter solutions
- Property owners and businesses in Lagos and across Nigeria

## 4. Key website pages
- Home
- About
- Services
- Projects
- Gallery
- FAQ
- Contact

## 5. Core features
- Multi-page responsive website
- Floating WhatsApp button
- Click-to-call button
- Service sections with clear calls to action
- Project descriptions
- Testimonials section
- Gallery section using images from the `img` folder
- SEO metadata on every page
- Schema markup for local search

## 6. Design direction
- Clean, modern, premium
- White or very light background
- Dark navy text
- Orange accent color
- Spacious layout
- Subtle shadows and borders
- No loud animations
- No emoji-heavy design
- Font Awesome icons only

## 7. Conversion strategy
The website should prioritize WhatsApp over contact form submission.

Primary CTA:
- Get a Free Quote on WhatsApp

Secondary CTA:
- Call Now

Optional contact form:
- Not required for launch
- Can be added later if needed

## 8. Content approach
- Write copy that sounds professional and human
- Use realistic sample testimonials during drafting
- Use realistic sample project descriptions during drafting
- Replace any unverified claims before launch
- Keep service explanations clear and simple

## 9. Success criteria
- The client likes the design
- The website looks premium
- The site loads quickly
- The site is easy to understand on mobile
- The site is ready for SEO and future content expansion
